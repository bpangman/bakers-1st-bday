🌊 Things to Do

30A is made for family fun — here's what's waiting for you

🏖️
Beach Days
30A's white sand beaches and calm turquoise waters are some of the best in the country. Perfect for little ones!
🚴
Bike the Timpoochee Trail
18.6 miles of paved coastal path. Baby trailers and bike seats available at local rental shops along the way.
🐢
30A Sea Life Discovery Center
Interactive programs in Grayton Beach: snorkeling, kayaking, and evening scavenger hunts. A hit with curious little minds!
🌟
Glow Paddle at WaterColor Boathouse
LED kayaks on Western Lake under the stars. A truly magical experience you won't find anywhere else!
🎨
Shacks Art (Grayton Beach)
Kids can make beach snow globes, enjoy face painting, and take art classes. A creative afternoon for the whole family!
🏊
Camp WaterColor
Slides, lazy river, pool cabanas, and food — if you're staying at WaterColor, this is Baker's first waterpark experience!
🌿
Grayton Beach State Park
Beautiful natural scenery, nature trails, and a rare coastal dune lake. One of the most scenic spots on the entire Gulf Coast.
🛍️
Seaside Town Center
Browse shops, catch live music at the amphitheater, and hit up the famous Airstream food trucks. Quintessential 30A!
